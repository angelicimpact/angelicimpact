Signature Training

See the Signs. Say Something. Save a Life.

A signature training that equips professionals and organizations to recognize indicators of abuse, neglect, trauma, risk, and system breakdown before concerns are minimized, missed, or misunderstood.

See the Signs™Risk recognition, documentation, reporting, and response.

What Leaders Gain

More than mandatory reporter training.

👁

Better Risk Recognition

Teams learn to recognize physical, behavioral, emotional, relational, sensory, and environmental indicators.

📝

Stronger Documentation

Participants practice clearer language for observations, concerns, patterns, and professional communication.

⚖

Clearer Reporting Confidence

Staff learn what to do when concern arises, how to act, and why waiting for certainty can be harmful.

Who This Is For

For every system that serves, protects, teaches, or responds to children.

DSS/CPS Teams

Supervisors, frontline staff, program managers, and child welfare leaders.

Schools

Teachers, counselors, administrators, social workers, and school staff.

First Responders

Law enforcement, fire, EMS, and crisis response professionals.

Courts & Advocates

GAL programs, child advocates, court staff, and multidisciplinary partners.
